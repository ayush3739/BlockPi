Everything You Need to Know

Does BlockPi track my browsing data?
Absolutely not. BlockPi is built on a privacy-first philosophy. All your browsing habits, timers, and rules are stored strictly on your local machine using chrome.storage.local. We have zero servers and zero interest in your data.
Will it slow down my browser?
No. Unlike many extensions that use heavy frameworks like React or Vue, BlockPi is written in pure Vanilla JavaScript. It has a zero-dependency architecture and its background service worker sleeps when you're not browsing tracked sites, ensuring near-zero CPU impact.
Is it really 100% free?
Yes. BlockPi is an open-source project created to help people reclaim their focus. There are no "Pro" versions, no hidden subscriptions, and no paid features. Everything you see is free to use forever.
Which browsers does it support?
BlockPi is built on the Manifest V3 standard, making it compatible with Google Chrome, Brave, Microsoft Edge, Opera, and most other Chromium-based browsers.
Can I whitelist specific sites?
Yes! You can manage your "Tracked Sites" list easily from the popup. If a site isn't on your list, BlockPi won't track it or block it, giving you complete control over your focus sessions.
How do I uninstall it?
Just like any other extension! Right-click the BlockPi icon in your toolbar and select "Remove from Chrome," or go to chrome://extensions and click "Remove" on the BlockPi card.
